Mel & Michel

Trattoria

Mel & Michelle is a small and intimate restaurant run by chef Nir Wayman. The chef infuse personal and artisanal touches on classical Italian cuisine.

Hand made gnocchi, fresh pasta and local produce from the various markets are the basis of the seasonal menu, which also incorporates truffle oils, roman artichokes and spices from around the globe.

White table cloths, soft- incandescent lighting and an attentive staff create the calm and welcoming ambiance, characteristic of Mel & Michelle.
